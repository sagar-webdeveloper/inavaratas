About Us

Optis is an IT Outsourcing and Software Solutions and Service provider

Optis is an IT Outsourcing and Software Solutions and Service provider With an indomitable spirit to succeed, Optis made a modest start in the year 2007 from a small office with a team of six. Today, with over a hundred people family, Optis has come a long way with continued inspiration that sustained business growth, nurturing relationship with our customers and fostering our skilled people for excellence in our works.

Our Line of Business (LOB) is strategically designed into 4Ps as Projects Services, Platform Solutions, Products and Professional Consulting Services; covering broadly Software Product Engineering, Application Development, Application Management, Mobile Application Development, Web Development, Cloud Engineering, Business Intelligence (BI) & Analytics, Enterprise Mobility, Social Media, Implementation and Support services on Microsoft Dynamics Axapta (AX), Microsoft Dynamics NAVISION (NAV), Microsoft Dynamics Great Plains (GP), Microsoft Dynamics CRM, Microsoft SharePoint, Open Source Content Management System (CMS), Business Applications as Point of Sale (POS) Solution, Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) Solution, Clinic & Healthcare Management Solution, Micro Finance Management Solution, Payroll Management Solution, Transport Management Solution, ERP for Agricultural & Farming, Architecture Consulting, Offshore Development Business Support, IT Infrastructure & Managed Services, Technical Staff Augmentation & Training services.

Our Vision

To be the competent and complete value added technology partner to our customers and be the admired Global software solutions & services provider in Information Technology space in our chosen markets.

Our Mission

To deliver the right business solutions that integrate People, Process and emerging Technology to help our customers achieve the business benefits of a collaborative enterprise at very affordable prices.
